Pakistan Odyssey - Explore the Beauty of Pakistan

Pakistan Tour Packages

Discover the entire country with visits to 8 iconic destinations, showcasing Pakistan's incredible diversity and culture.

1st Destination

Hunza Valley

Nestled in the Karakoram Range, Hunza Valley offers breathtaking scenery, including the majestic Rakaposhi Peak and Attabad Lake.

2nd Destination

Swat Valley

Often called the "Switzerland of Pakistan," Swat Valley boasts stunning landscapes, ancient Buddhist ruins, and the picturesque Mahodand Lake.

3rd Destination

Naran Valley

A haven for nature lovers, Naran Valley features lush green valleys, cascading waterfalls, and the serene Saiful Muluk Lake.

4th Destination

Gilgit-Baltistan

Home to the Karakoram Highway, Gilgit-Baltistan offers a diverse landscape, including glaciers, mountains, and the ancient city of Gilgit.

5th Destination

Lahore

The cultural heart of Pakistan, Lahore is renowned for its historical sites, including the Badshahi Mosque and the Lahore Fort, as well as its vibrant food scene.

6th Destination

Islamabad

The modern capital of Pakistan, Islamabad boasts well-planned architecture, lush green parks, and the iconic Faisal Mosque.

7th Destination

Karachi

The bustling metropolis of Pakistan, Karachi offers a unique blend of old and new, with historical sites like the Mohatta Palace and modern attractions like the Clifton Beach.

8th Destination

Mohenjo-daro

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, Mohenjo-daro is the remains of an ancient Indus Valley Civilization city, offering a glimpse into Pakistan's rich history.
